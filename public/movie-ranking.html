The Best Movies According to Taha Dhamman Wala

My top 5 movies of all-time are:

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Ex Machina

Really cool sci-fi movie.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.

Avatar

Superb story & tremendous VFX execution.

The Fault in Our Stars

I am falling short of words to describe this Mind-blowing film and novel. What a story!!!! Great Performances by all the cast. The story is very beautiful and heartbreaking at the same time.
